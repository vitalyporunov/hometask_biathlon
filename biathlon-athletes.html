Top Biathlon Athletes

Champions of Skiing and Shooting

Johannes Thingnes Bø

Nationality: Norway

Known for his explosive speed on the tracks and pinpoint accuracy on the shooting range, Johannes Thingnes Bø is a dominant force in men's biathlon. With numerous Olympic and World Championship titles under his belt, he's a true icon of the sport.

Key Achievements:

  • Olympic Gold: 2018 (Individual, Sprint, Relay)
  • World Champion: 2019, 2020 (Multiple events)

Dorotea Wierer

Nationality: Italy

Known for her unwavering consistency and tactical brilliance, Dorotea Wierer is a celebrated figure in women's biathlon. With a charismatic personality and a knack for delivering under pressure, she's amassed an impressive collection of Olympic and World Championship medals.

Key Achievements:

  • Olympic Bronze: 2014 (Mixed Relay)
  • World Champion: 2019, 2020 (Mass Start)
  • Overall World Cup Winner: 2016, 2019, 2020

Julia Simon

Nationality: France

A dynamic and aggressive competitor, Julia Simon brings a youthful energy to women's biathlon. Her lightning-fast skiing and fearless approach to the shooting range have catapulted her to the top ranks of the sport, earning her numerous World Cup victories and podium finishes.

Key Achievements:

  • World Cup Winner: 2022/2023
  • World Championship Silver: 2023 (Pursuit)

Eduard Latypov

Nationality: Russia

A rising star in men's biathlon, Eduard Latypov combines technical proficiency with a calm and collected demeanor. His consistent performances and impressive shooting accuracy have earned him a reputation as a serious contender for major titles.

Key Achievements:

  • Olympic Silver: 2022 (Relay)
  • World Championship Silver: 2021 (Relay)
  • Multiple World Cup podium finishes
